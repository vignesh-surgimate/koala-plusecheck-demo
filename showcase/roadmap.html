Roadmap & Business Value

Where the platform stands today, what's next, and why it matters in business terms.

Business Value

📉 Lower cost of ownership

Single-owner locators, SOLID structure and two documented dedup passes mean every fix lands in exactly one place. Zero TypeScript/lint errors and 284 generated doc pages keep the maintenance bus-factor high and onboarding to hours, not weeks.

🚀 Faster release cycles

16× parallel capacity (workers × shards), a 5-minute smoke gate per PR, and no-browser console gates that fail fast before a single browser launches. The nightly covers the entire customer base — 20 tenant/role legs — unattended.

🧭 Leadership control without engineering

The Runner Manager hands run scope, priority and data volume to QA leadership via SQLite. Re-prioritizing tonight's regression is a table edit — no commit, no review, no wait.

🏥 Compliance-grade evidence

Per-run action logs with ET timestamps, screenshots/videos/traces on failure, env-stamped Allure history and ELK trends — the audit trail healthcare conversations expect, produced automatically.
